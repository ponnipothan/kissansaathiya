🏛️ Government Subsidy Schemes

⚠️ For accurate eligibility and application support, please contact your local Village Agriculture Officer.

🌾 Rythu Bharosa (Telangana)

Benefit: ₹5,000 per acre per season

Description: Direct investment support for Rabi & Kharif seasons.

Focus: Input cost reduction

🛡️ Rythu Bima

Benefit: ₹5 lakh life insurance

Description: Covers farmers aged 18–59 with full premium paid by government.

Focus: Family security

💰 PM-KISAN (Central)

Benefit: ₹6,000/year

Description: ₹2,000 paid every 4 months to farmers.

Focus: Financial assistance

🚜 Farm Mechanization Subsidy

Benefit: 50% – 80% subsidy

Description: Support for tractors, tillers & modern equipment.

Focus: Modernization

💧 Micro Irrigation (PMKSY)

Benefit: Up to 90% subsidy

Description: Drip & sprinkler irrigation support.

Focus: Water conservation
